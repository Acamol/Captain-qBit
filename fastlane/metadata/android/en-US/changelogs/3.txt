First release of Captain qBit.

- Remotely view, add, pause/resume, and delete torrents on your qBittorrent server
- Sort, search, and filter by status, category, tracker, or tags
- Add magnets or .torrent files with category, save path, and start-paused options
- Per-torrent details with a browsable file tree, trackers, and peers
- Works with qBittorrent 4.x and 5.x
- Persistent status notification with live speeds
